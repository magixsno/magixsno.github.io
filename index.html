Knowledge Engine - Google Search

I am a full-stack software engineer on knowledge engine, the fact-serving engine on Google Search. The project within knowledge engine I am working on is purposed with the high velocity end-to-end serving of facts onto Google Search with as minimal engineering effort as possible. This means the development automatic smart tooling for, techniques for discovery of data, automating extraction of structured data, addition of such data into the Knowledge Graph, analysis of query intent of the data, formatting of the data, and UI display of the data on Search.

List Carousels - Knowledge Engine

My first outward facing, public project on knowledge engine is the surfacing of like data for collection style queries. These are surfaced for a few queries as a list carousel at the top of the search page, for example, disney princesses and zodiac signs.

ATLAS Experiment

My lust for knowledge is seeded deep in my curiousity of nature. During my time in University, I spent some research efforts on the TTH ATLAS project at CERN. The TTH, lead by Dr. Peter Onyisi, is tasked with determining the probability of a top & anti-top quark collision yielding a Higgs Boson. This project involved building complex linux stacks to run cern software to train large, complex models for identification of such collisions.

FFL2 Mission Patch

Fruit Fly Lab 2 (FFL2) is an experiment run by NASA on the ISS. As part of this mission, they are sending a payload to space with flies, food, and equipment to the ISS. This design, initially inspired from Dr. Sanjoy Som, is part of the payload going to the ISS. Due to insufficient funding for the project, the official launch date of this payload is roughly set for Summer 2017.
